Please take a moment to read through the process carefully:
  • To help me understand your project better, kindly describe it in as much detail as possible — including any specific requirements (e.g. character descriptions, art style, canvas size, etc.).
  •  After receiving your brief, I’ll start with rough sketch(es) for your approval before moving on to the next steps.
  •  I’ll keep you updated regularly during the process so we can make adjustments early on. It saves time and avoids reworking details at the final stage. (For example, changing a small part of a creature’s body means adjusting shape → lineart → base colors → shading/effects…)
  • You're also welcome to test my skills first if you’d like to assess compatibility.
  •   Payment terms: Please make a 25–50% upfront payment once i starting the rough sketch(es). The remaining 50–75% can be paid upon project completion. We can also arrange payment in milestones or as a lump sum — whichever works best for you.
  • After the final payment is received, I’ll send you the original high-resolution file(s) without watermarks.
